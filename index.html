About Me

Hey! My name’s Brian, but most folks call me BP. I’m an eclectic web developer with a strong eye for aesthetics who delights in bringing ideas and designs to digital life.

I live outside of Boston in a small city called Waltham with my wonderful and loving wife, incredibly adorable daughter, and a bull-headed bulldog who emphasizes the breed’s stubborn nature. When not working you can find me (hopefully not actually find me) somewhere outdoors with dirt on my hands, casting a line or treading the trails.
